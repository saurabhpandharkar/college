Department profile

The department of microbiology was established in 1991 with the aim of offering a structured course for students keen on pursuing a career in the subject.
Headed by Prof. Dr. S. T. Pawar, the department has much success to its credit, least of all being the success rate of 80%.
The curriculum is revised regularly thus bringing the latest and the best knowledge to students.
This is backed by a well-trained teaching and non-teaching staff as well as numerous books and journals in the library.
