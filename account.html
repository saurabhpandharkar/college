Department profile

Accountancy is the practice of recording, allocating and outlining on trade transactions for a business.
It provides an assessment of management concerning the financial outcomes and ranking of an establishment.
A few accountancy tasks include recording, classification and reporting of a transaction.
When any information is prepared as a report for any individuals or companies to be used outside the organization, the process is known as ‘financial accounting’.
